Hobyah input file for plotting the volume flows in tunnels, volume flows
of trains and in the annulus around trains.  It was written to check
whether the calculation of annulus airflows in SESconv.py is correct.

  version 1

  runtype plot
  header underfoot
  QA1 4
  QA2 Hobyah documentation images
  QA3 Files that generate images used in the documentation
end settings

  normal SES-062-trains-crossing.sbn
end files
